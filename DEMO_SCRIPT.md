# Demo Video Script (1–3 minutes)

Use this script with any text-to-speech tool (e.g., MiniMax) or record your own voice.
Estimated duration: ~2 minutes

---

## [INTRO — 15 seconds]

"Hi, this is a demo of my Coffee Market Analysis Dashboard, built for the ACC102 Mini Assignment, Track 4.

This interactive tool helps users explore global coffee commodity trends — from price movements to production patterns and international trade."

---

## [OVERVIEW — 20 seconds]

"When you open the app, you land on the Overview page. Here you can see key metrics at a glance: the latest Arabica price, the ten-year average, global production volume, and the number of top producing countries.

Below the metrics, there are two summary charts — a price trend line and a pie chart showing market share by country."

---

## [PRICE TRENDS — 25 seconds]

"Clicking on Price Trends in the sidebar takes you to the price analysis page. You can filter by year range and choose to view Arabica, Robusta, or both. The chart is fully interactive — you can hover to see exact values and zoom into specific periods.

Notice the spike around 2021 to 2022 — that reflects supply chain disruptions and adverse weather conditions in Brazil. Below the chart, there is a summary table with annual averages, volatility, and year-over-year changes."

---

## [PRODUCTION ANALYSIS — 25 seconds]

"The Production Analysis page lets you explore coffee output by country and region. You can select a year and see a horizontal bar chart ranking the top producers. Brazil leads by a large margin, followed by Vietnam and Colombia.

You can also select multiple countries to compare their production trends over time, and see a regional breakdown pie chart."

---

## [TRADE & COUNTRY EXPLORER — 25 seconds]

"The Trade page shows international export patterns. South America consistently has the highest net export surplus. You can also compare export-to-production ratios across countries.

The Country Explorer lets you pick any country and see its key statistics, production trend, and regional comparison — great for a focused deep dive."

---

## [KEY INSIGHTS — 20 seconds]

"The Key Insights page summarizes the main findings: Brazil's dominance, the 2021 price spike, South America's trade leadership, emerging producers like Ethiopia, and the inverse relationship between GDP per capita and coffee export dependency."

---

## [OUTRO — 15 seconds]

"The app is built with Python, Streamlit, and Plotly. The full analysis workflow, including data cleaning and descriptive statistics, is documented in the accompanying Jupyter notebook.

Thank you for watching."

---

## Recording Tips

1. Open the Streamlit app in your browser
2. Use a screen recorder (OBS, Loom, or browser extension)
3. Follow the script while clicking through each page
4. Keep it smooth — practice once before recording
5. Export as MP4 and upload to a platform accessible to the marker
